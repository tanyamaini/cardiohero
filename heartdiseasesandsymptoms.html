Heart Diseases and Symptoms

Coronary Heart Disease

  • Shortness of breath
  • Palpitations
  • Weakness of diziness
  • Nausea
  • Sweating

Heart Attack

  • Discomfort, pressure, heaviness, or pain in chest, arm, or below the breastbone
  • Discomfort radiating to the back, jaw, throat, or arm
  • Fullness, indigestion, or choking feeling
  • Sweating, nausea, vomiting, or dizziness
  • Extreme weakness, anxiety, or shortness of breath
  • Rapid or irregular heartbeats

Arrythmias

  • Palpitations
  • Pounding in chest or chest discomfort
  • Dizziness or feeling light-headed
  • Fainting
  • Shortness of breath
  • Shortness of breath

Atrial Fibrillation

  • Palpitation
  • Sluggishness
  • Dizziness
  • Chest discomfort
  • Shortness of breath

Heart Valve Disease

  • Shortness of breath and/or difficulty catching breath
  • Weakness or dizziness
  • Discomfort in chest
  • Palpitations

Heart Failure

  • Shortness of breath especially when lying down flat
  • Cough that produces white sputum
  • Rapid weight gain
  • Swelling in ankles, legs, and abdomen
  • Dizziness
  • Fatigue and weakness
  • Rapid or irregular heartbeats
  • Nausea, palpitations, and chest pain

Heart Muscle Disease

  • Chest pain or pressure
  • Swelling of lower extremities
  • Fatigue
  • Fainting
  • Palipitations
  • Symptoms of heart failure (See above)

Pericarditis

  • Sharp pain located in the center of the chest that may spread to the neck and arms, and that is made worse when lying down and relieved by sitting (Different from all other chest pain)
  • Low-grade fever
  • Increased heart rate
